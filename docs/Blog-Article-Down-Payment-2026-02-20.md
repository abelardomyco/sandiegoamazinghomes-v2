## Structure used

1. **Opening** — Direct answer: you don’t need 20%; 3–5% conventional/FHA is possible; what matters is price range, competition, and monthly comfort.
2. **Reality check** — Misconception that you need six figures/20% down; tradeoffs (PMI, competing offers); reframe to “what can I afford per month?”
3. **San Diego–specific breakdown** — Coastal/prestige (La Jolla, Del Mar, Coronado, Rancho Santa Fe); urban/central (Mission Valley, North Park, Little Italy); South Bay/East County (Chula Vista, Eastlake, Bonita, Otay Ranch, La Mesa); North County (Poway, Rancho Bernardo, Carmel Valley, Encinitas, Carlsbad). Price ranges and example down-payment amounts; link to market page and neighborhoods.
4. **Practical guidance** — Four steps: get pre-approved; decide what you’re optimizing for; narrow by neighborhood; know the competition. Links to neighborhoods and homes.
5. **Local insight** — One market vs. many; matching down payment and loan type to the right submarket; buyers who stopped waiting for 20%.
6. **Conclusion** — Short summary + soft CTA: explore listings, market dashboard, connect with Rosamelia.
